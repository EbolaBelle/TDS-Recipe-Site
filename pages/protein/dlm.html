Duck Liver Mousse

Ingredients

  • 30lbs duck liver
  • 236g salt
  • 65g pink curing salt
  • 20 shallots (sliced)
  • 25g white pepper
  • 50g juniper
  • 10g ginger powder
  • 10g whole clove
  • 10g allspice
  • 30 bay leaf
  • 50 sprigs thyme (picked if woody)
  • 30 sprigs of oregan (picked if woody)
  • 1000g brandy (prefer Mister Boston)

Process

  1. Clean livers of sinew and fat. Soak in buttermilk overnight if available, then drain
  2. Sweat shallot until translucent in a large rondo with a little oil.
  3. Add spices, sweat until fragrant
  4. Add brandy and flame until alcohol is gone
  5. Add herbs and sweat until fragrant
  6. Add salt and pink curing salt last
  7. Scrape into a hotel pan to cool
  8. Once the cure mix is cool, add to livers and mix thoroughly
  9. Cure for two days, turning daily to ensure even cure distribution
  10. Portion into 2lb bags and seal, hold in the freezer
  11. Sous vide at 145F for 1hr
  12. Blend with 1lb cold butter chunks per 2lb bag
  13. Pass through a chinois and hold in pint containers (put plastic wrap on top to prevent oxidation
