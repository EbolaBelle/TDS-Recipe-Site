Ingredients

  • 30 duck breast
  • 90g pink salt
  • 30g bay leaf
  • 20g dry marjoram
  • 30g juniper
  • 30g pepper
  • 60g garlic
  • salt

Process

  1. Blend together in robo
  2. Rub all over breasts and place in a container
  3. Add enough salt so they are completely covered
  4. refrigerate for 2 days
  5. Rinse and hang
