Croquettes

Ingredients

  • 10lbs beef cheeks
  • 1 bottle red wine
  • 2 onions, cut in half
  • 1 bunch thyme
  • 2 carrots, cut into thirds
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into thirds
  • 2oz fresh garlic
  • 1/2C flour
  • 3oz red wine vinger
  • 2oz sugar

Process

  1. Braise cheeks in wine and mirepoix with enough water to cover
  2. Remove cheeks from liquid and discard the thyme
  3. Put veg blender with garlic. Blend with a bit of the liquid
  4. Add flour and continue blending
  5. Reduce liquid to 2 cups
  6. Add vegetable blend + vinegar and sugar, bring to simmer
  7. Pour gravy over beef cheeks and thoroughly combine
  8. Add salt, lots of salt. More than you think you need. Taste and adjust seasoning, including vinegar and sugar. It should be about 10% saltier than what would be considered well salted.
