Fluke Cure

Ingredients

  • 50% salt
  • 50% sugar
  • Lemon zest

Process

  1. Remove fish skin
  2. Add cure to the bottom of a hotel pan and place fish on top. Add enough cure to cover the fish and pack down.
  3. Cure for 30mins in refrigerator
  4. Rinse and pat the fish dry with cold water
  5. Remove any stringy bits from the middle of the flatfish
  6. Seal in vac bag and store under cooler/freezer
  7. Hand chop fish for service
