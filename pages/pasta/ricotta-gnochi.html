Ricotta Gnocchi

Ricotta

Ingredients

  • 2 gallons milk
  • 12 oz. white vinegar

Process

  1. Heat milk to 180 degrees. Stir regularly to avoid scorching
  2. Remove from heat, stir in vinegar
  3. Cool for 15min, then strain through double layer cheese cloth
  4. Put weight on top and let strain overnight

Spinach Puree

Ingredients

  • 1 bag baby spinach

Process

  1. Blanch spinach, then shock
  2. Drain spinach, squeeze out as much water as possible through a linen
  3. Puree spinach with just enough eggs to make a smooth and thick puree, it will need manipulation with a spatula

Gnocchi

Ingredients

  • 32oz ricotts
  • 20oz flour
  • 11oz spinach puree
  • 1.5oz salt

Process

  1. Mix with dough hook into a smooth dough
  2. Let rest in cooler for at least an hour
  3. Roll out; cut on a bias
