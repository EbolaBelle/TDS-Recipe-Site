Semolina Gnocchi

Ingredients

  • 26oz milk
  • 2oz butter
  • 12oz semolina
  • 1 egg
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt

Process

  1. Bring milk, butter, salt, and nutmeg to a simmer.
  2. Add semolina and incorporate
  3. Add egg and quickly mix
  4. Cook like "pate a choux" until the dough is smooth and separates from the sides
  5. Roll out while the dough is still hot
