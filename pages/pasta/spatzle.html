Spätzle

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • 2qt rye flour
  • 2qt HG flour
  • 1/4C kosher salt
  • 2C grainy mustard
  • 4C milk

Process

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, water, mustard, and salt in a large mixing bowl
  2. Whisk in dry ingredients
  3. 1/4 fill a deep hotel pan with water. Place perforated hotel pan on top and boil on range
  4. Working in small batches, use a metal bench scraper to push raw dough through a perforated pan into the large hotel pan underneath and boil spätzle in water for ~30 seconds.
  5. Use a spider to fish out the pasta and onto an oiled parchment lined baking sheet to cool.
  6. Make sure to oil the pasta and mix so it does not stick together. Store in a cambro container.
