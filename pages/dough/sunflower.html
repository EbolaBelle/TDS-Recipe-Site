Sunflower Oat Bread

Ingredients

  • Sourdough - 1041g (20%)
  • White - 3124g (60%)
  • WW/Rye - 2083g (40%)
  • Salt - 104g (2%)
  • Water - 3801g (73%)
  • Honey - 260g (5%)
  • Sunflower Seeds - 961g (18%)
  • Whole Oats - 961g (18%)
  • Water - 2083g (40%)
  • Salt - 73g (1%)

Process

  1. Boil water for soaker and pour over whole oats and salt for soaker, let soak for at least two hours (preferably overnight).
  2. Toast sunflower seeds and add to soaker (not necessary to soak as long)
  3. Mix flours and water and leave to autolyse for at least one hour.
  4. Add soaker and all remaining ingredients to the dough and mix on low speed for 10mins.
  5. Let rest and mix for an additional 6mins on second speed.
  6. If dough is cohering (it will not clear the bowl) move to bus tub.
  7. Fold at least 5x and allow to ferment at room temperature for at least 5hrs.
  8. Portion in mixing bowl at 2400g and add to loaf pan (generously pan spray pans).
  9. Proof overnight until the dough does not spring back when pressed down.
  10. Bake at 400 degrees for ~75mins until internal temperature reaches 200, turning in the oven as necessary.
