Brioche Loaf Bread

Ingredients

  • 1872g HG flour
  • 240g wheat flour
  • 324g starter
  • 51g salt
  • 36g yeast
  • 252g sugar
  • 7 eggs
  • 510g milk
  • 270g butter
  • 960g water

Process

  1. Standard mixing method, but mix on high speed until dough is cohesive and pulls away from the bowl. Could take a long time.
  2. Divide into 3 loaf pans
