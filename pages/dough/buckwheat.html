VG/GF Buckwheat Crackers

Ingredients

  • 1C buckwheat grains
  • 1/2C almond flour
  • 1T ground flax seeds
  • 2tbsp whole flax seeds
  • 2t whole caraway seeds
  • 2t raw cacao powder
  • 1T sesame seeds
  • 1t kosher salt
  • 1/4C cold water

Process

  1. Soak buckwheat groats overnight. Drain soaking liquid
  2. Grind flax seeds in blender, then add 3T hot water.
  3. To the blender add 1/4C cold water, buckwheat, cacao, and salt
  4. Combine remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl
  5. Spread mixture onto a greased silpat and bake at 200 degrees for 20mins
  6. Score the crackers to create the final shape
  7. Bake longer to remove excess liquid if needed, then cool.
  8. Dehydrate for 12hrs at 130F
