Tart Dough

Ingredients

  • 4lbs Butter (grated and frozen)
  • 1816g Flour
  • 850g Ice Water
  • 120g Salt

Process

  1. Combine butter, salt, and flour in a mixer with the dough hook. Slowly add water and continue mixing until the dough is fully formed, nice and smooth.
  2. Form the dough into rectangle shape. Cover and let the dough rest in cooler for an hour/
  3. Roll out the dough into a long strip and fold into thirds like a letter. Cover and let rest in the cooler for another hour.
  4. Cut into thirds lengthwise and roll to the width of the pasto roller. Start at the highest setting and roll pasta dough down to the 7.
  5. Cut into 14in sheets and put on a well sprayed piece of parchment. Spray both sides. Continue with the rest of the dough.
