Brioche

Ingredients

  • HG Flour - 1800g (90%)
  • Wheat Flour - 200g (10%)
  • Dry Milk 168g - (8.4%)
  • Sugar - 120g (6%)
  • Yeast - 26g (1.3%)
  • Eggs - 1000g 50%
  • Cold Butter - 860g (43%)
  • Water - 342g (17.1%)
  • Salt - 45g (2.25%)

Process

  1. Mix all ingredients (except butter) until they clear the bowl and gluten is well developed (7-8mins).
  2. Add butter and mix on high until fully incorpated (5-6mins).
  3. Move to container for bulk ferment and refrigerate overnight before use.
  4. Portion 100g balls and proof until ready to bake.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 15mins, turning once.
