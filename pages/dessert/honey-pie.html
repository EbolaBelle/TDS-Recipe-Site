Ginger Snap Crust

Ingredients

  • 444g Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/2C Molasses
  • 340g Soft Butter
  • 2T Ground Giner
  • 2t Ground Cinnamon
  • 4t Baking Soda
  • 1t Salt
  • 4C Flout

Process

  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs on at a time, then molasses.
  3. Scrape down sides.
  4. Add remaining ingredients and mix until combined for cookie dough.
  5. Press out in circle on tray and bake for 30min at 350.

Honey Pie Custard

Ingredients

  • 1C Cream
  • 7 Yolks
  • 5T Melted Butter
  • 2t Vanilla
  • 3/4C Honey
  • 1 Orange, zested
  • 1/4C Brown Sugar
  • 2T Flour
  • 1C Crème Fraiche
  • 1t Salt (adjust TT)

Process

  1. In stand mixer: whip salt, sugar, flour, and butter until fully combined.
  2. Add yolks one at a time, whipping in fully before the next.
  3. Add cream + honey, mix until fully combined and smooth
  4. Cook over double boiler until custard comes together.
  5. Cool, then whip in crème fraiche.
  6. Pour in a shell, bake for 15mins, and check. You can also just pour the mixture in a shell and bake for 50-65mins at the same temp.
