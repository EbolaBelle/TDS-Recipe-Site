Ingredients

  • 2 Egg Whites
  • 2T Extract of Choice
  • 5C Pecans/Walnuts (440g)
  • 2C Sugar
  • 1/2t Salt
  • 3t Cinnamon
  • 1/2t Cayenne

Process

  1. Preheat overn to 225.
  2. Combine dry ingredients (not nuts).
  3. Combine egg whites/extract, beat until fluffy.
  4. Toss pecans until fully coated with the beaten whites.
  5. Add dry ingredients and toss until coated.
  6. Spread out on baking sheet with parchment paper.
  7. Baking for 60mins, turning every 20min.
